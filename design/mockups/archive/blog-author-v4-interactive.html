About Lena

I've spent 12 years planning African safaris and writing about the continent I love. Every article is built on first-hand experience — I've slept in the camps, driven the roads, and watched the sunsets.

“My aim is to help you travel smarter, more deeply, and more responsibly.”

From the rust-red dunes of Namibia to the emerald canopy of Rwanda, I've built relationships with the lodges, guides, and conservancies that make African travel transformative. Whether it's your first safari or your tenth, I write to help you see more, understand more, and tread more lightly.
