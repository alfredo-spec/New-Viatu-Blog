Blog component design

"At a Glance" review box — five concepts

Each concept is designed to sit early in a review or trip showcase blog post — after the intro paragraph, before the deep dive. All use real sample data from a Londolozi Founders Camp review. Shown in a simulated blog content flow.

Concept 1

The Passport Stamp

Classic left-accent bar with a two-column data grid. Familiar, scannable, and elegant. The most conservative option — blends easily into editorial flow.
