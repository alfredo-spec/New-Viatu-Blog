Image Gallery Element

Six layout variants for embedding photo galleries within Viatu blog posts. Each variant is shown inside a simulated 720px content column.

6 variants
1

Single Inline

Contained within the 720px content column. border-radius: var(--rlg) aspect-ratio: 16/9 box-shadow: var(--sh-sm)

2

Single Full-Bleed

Breaks out to full viewport width. width: 100vw aspect-ratio: 21/9 No border-radius. Overlaid caption with gradient.

3

2-Up Bordered

Two-column grid within content column. gap: 16px aspect-ratio: 3/2 border-radius: var(--rlg) Hover: scale(1.02) Mobile: single column.

4

2-Up Full-Bleed

Full viewport width, tight grid. gap: 4px aspect-ratio: 4/3 No border-radius. Individual overlaid captions. Mobile: single column, no gap.

5

3-Up Bordered

Three-column grid within content column. gap: 16px aspect-ratio: 1/1 border-radius: var(--rlg) Hover: scale(1.02) Tablet: tighter gap. Mobile: single column.

6

3-Up Full-Bleed

Full viewport width, no gaps, images touch. aspect-ratio: 3/4 No border-radius. Overlaid captions. Hover: brightness(1.05) Mobile: single column, 2px gap.
