# Facility Icons — Visual Descriptions for AI Generation

Each icon is described with concrete drawable shapes for the Viatu icon system (24×24 grid, stroke-only outline, `stroke-width: 1.5`, `stroke-linecap: round`, `stroke-linejoin: round`).

---

**1. Bar / Lounge** — `bar-lounge.svg`
A cocktail glass tilted slightly with a small round olive or cherry on a toothpick resting across the rim. Single continuous outline.

**2. Secured Parking** — `secured-parking.svg`
A capital letter "P" inside a rounded square, with a small closed padlock sitting on the top-right corner of the square.

**3. Free Parking** — `free-parking.svg`
A capital letter "P" centered inside a circle. Clean and simple, no additional elements.

**4. Outdoor Pool** — `outdoor-pool.svg`
A top-down rectangular pool shape with rounded corners, two horizontal wavy lines inside representing water. A small circle (sun) in the upper-right corner with two short radiating lines.

**5. Restaurant** — `restaurant.svg`
A fork on the left and a knife on the right, both vertical, with a small plate (circle) centered between them at mid-height.

**6. Room Service** — `room-service.svg`
A domed cloche (half-circle dome with a small knob on top) sitting on a horizontal tray line. Clean side profile view.

**7. Spa** — `spa.svg`
A single lotus flower viewed from the front — three layered petal shapes fanning upward from a curved base line. Symmetrical.

**8. Wi-Fi** — `wifi.svg`
Three concentric arc lines radiating upward from a small filled dot at the bottom center. Classic wireless signal icon.

**9. Tour / Sightseeing Desk** — `tour-sightseeing-desk.svg`
A compass rose: a circle with a four-pointed directional star inside, with a small dot at center. North pointer slightly emphasized.

**10. Laundry Service** — `laundry-service.svg`
A front-loading washing machine: rounded square outer shell with a large circle in the center (the door/drum) and a small horizontal strip across the top (control panel) with two tiny dots for buttons.

**11. Wheelchair Access** — `wheelchair-access.svg`
The international wheelchair symbol: a simplified seated figure in a wheelchair shown in profile, with a large wheel circle at the back and a smaller wheel at the front.

**12. BBQ / Picnic Area** — `bbq-picnic-area.svg`
A round kettle grill seen from the side: a dome-shaped lid on top, a bowl-shaped base, and two short angled legs. Three short vertical wavy lines rising from the lid (smoke/heat).

**13. 24-Hour Front Desk** — `24-hour-front-desk.svg`
A bell (service bell) with a small round button on top, sitting on a flat base line. A tiny "24" text or a small clock face beside it.

**14. Children's Play Area** — `childrens-play-area.svg`
A simple swing set: an A-frame structure (inverted V) with a single seat hanging from the crossbar on two lines. Small and geometric.

**15. Business Centre** — `business-centre.svg`
A briefcase: a rounded rectangle body with a small handle (half-rounded rectangle) on top center, and a horizontal line dividing the case into upper and lower halves.

**16. Library** — `library.svg`
An open book seen from above: two rectangular pages angled apart like butterfly wings, connected at a center spine line. Clean and symmetrical.

**17. Currency Exchange** — `currency-exchange.svg`
Two overlapping circles (like a Venn diagram) with a dollar sign "$" in one and a generic currency symbol in the other. A small curved arrow arcing between them.

**18. Elevators** — `elevators.svg`
A pair of elevator doors: two tall adjacent rectangles with a small gap between them (the door seam). A small up-arrow and down-arrow centered above the doors.

**19. Heated Pool** — `heated-pool.svg`
A rectangular pool shape with rounded corners and two horizontal wavy lines inside (water). Three short vertical wavy lines rising from the top edge (steam/heat).

**20. Conference Facilities** — `conference-facilities.svg`
A rectangular table seen from above with four to six small circles arranged around it (chairs/seats). Clean top-down view.

**21. Gym** — `gym.svg`
A horizontal barbell: a straight bar with a circular weight plate on each end. Simple and symmetrical.

**22. Free Airport Shuttle** — `free-airport-shuttle.svg`
A minibus/van in side profile: a rounded rectangular body, two circles at bottom (wheels), and a windshield line. A small airplane silhouette above or on the van's side.

**23. Courtesy Car** — `courtesy-car.svg`
A sedan car in side profile: a smooth body with a cabin roofline, two circle wheels, and a windshield. Minimal detail, clean outline.

**24. Indoor Pool** — `indoor-pool.svg`

**25. Hairdresser / Barber** — `hairdresser-barber.svg`

**26. Flush Toilet** — `flush-toilet.svg`

**27. Battery Charging** — `battery-charging.svg`

**28. Bucket Shower** — `bucket-shower.svg`

**29. Hot Water Shower** — `hot-water-shower.svg`

**35. Communal Dining** — `communal-dining.svg`

**36. Kiddies Club** — `kiddies-club.svg`

**37. Private Vehicle** — `private-vehicle.svg`

**38. Eco Friendly** — `eco-friendly.svg`
